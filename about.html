About Us

Founded by Isabella, Aureme Jewelry is the embodiment of feminine empowerment and elegance. Born in a traditional Chinese family that valued sons over daughters, Isabella grew up experiencing gender inequality firsthand. As she matured, she embraced feminism and dreamed of creating a space where women could reveal their own beauty and confidence.

Aureme Jewelry uses eco-friendly, recyclable materials, focusing on alloys, 925 silver, cubic zirconia, and moissanite to craft each piece with care and intention.
